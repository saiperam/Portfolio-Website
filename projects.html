MedVisor

1st Place Finalist for AIM Night, Fall 2024

MedVisor is an AI-powered solution developed to detect and classify lumbar spine degenerative diseases from MRI scans using advanced deep learning techniques: U-Net for disc segmentation and a ResNet-50 for disease classification.

QuadCore

QuadCore is an AI-powered financial application designed to revolutionize personal finance management. Featuring four advanced AI models, it empowers users with a personalized budget advisor, secure transaction monitoring, a financial chatbot, and a stock investment analyzer. From budgeting to investments, QuadCore leverages cutting-edge technology like Linear Regression, Random Forest Classifiers, and DistilGPT to deliver real-time insights, detect anomalies, and provide expert financial advice at your fingertips.

SpeakWise

I built the backend for SpeakWise, an app that improves public speaking by providing real-time feedback using Firebase authentication and OpenAI’s language model. The app leveraged NLP and speech recognition for sentiment analysis, refining users’ delivery while ensuring scalability and performance.
